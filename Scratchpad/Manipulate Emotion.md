>[!info]+ Base (Level 1)
> - Tug on a person's emotions
> - This allows you to raise a certain emotion, feeling, or state of mind
> - Using this ability grants a +1 to any conversational check against that person that pertains to the enflamed emotion
> - The person has a +3 chance to notice if they're suspicious of you, +2 if they're neutral, or +1 if they're friendly. 

>[!info]+ Trained (Level 2)
> - You learn to use **Manipulate Emotion** at three different strengths: subtle, normal, or vivid.
> - Subtle provides +1 to a conversational check
> - Normal provides a +2
> - Vivid provides a +3
> - The target has a +3 chance to notice if they're suspicious of you, +2 if they're neutral, or +1 if they're friendly when using a subtle strength.
> - The target gains a +1 bonus if using a normal strength, and +2 bonus at vivid strength

>[!info]+ Honed (Level 3)
> - You learn to manipulate emotions at an advanced level, tugging at the frayed ends that already exist.
> - The target has a +1 chance to notice if they're suspicious of you, +0 if they're neutral, or -1 if they're friendly when using a subtle strength.

>[!info]+ Expert (Level 4)
> - You master the skill of manipulating emotions.
> - A subtle manipulation provides a +3 bonus
> - A normal manipulation provides a +5 bonus
> - A strong manipulation provides a +7 bonus

>[!info]+ Savant (Level 5)
> - You lean how to enflame emotions to such a degree that even the aware can't escape you. 
> - When you use a strong manipulation, you may choose to push the manipulation further and add an additional +5 to the bonus. 
> - The target will be aware of the manipulation once the effect wears off, but not while it's happening if they fail their save.
